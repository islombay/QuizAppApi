[CLIENT - API]

GET     /api/subjects                       --- list of all subjects without questions
GET     /api/subjects/:id                   --- specific subject with questions
GET     /api/subjects/:id/questions         --- list of all questions
GET     /api/subjects/:id/answer            --- answers for questions

[ADMIN]
POST    /api/admin/subject                  --- add new subject
PUT     /api/admin/subject                  --- update the existing subject
DELETE  /api/admin/subject                  --- delete the existing subject

PUT     /api/admin/question                 --- update the existing question of the subject
DELETE  /api/admin/question                 --- delete the existing question of the subject
POST    /api/admin/question                 --- add question to the subject
